Frozen Berry Yoghurt

Description

Three ingredient, low calorie, low-fat, high in protien frozen yoghurt treat

Ingredients:

  • 250g Frozen mixed berry
  • 250 Greek yoghurt
  • 1 Tbsp honey or agave syrup

Steps:

  1. Blend berries, yoghurt and honey or agave syrup in a food processor for 20 seconds.
  2. Scoop into bowls and serve
